Chicken Tikka Masala

  • Time: 0Hours 45Mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
extra-virgin olive oil 1 tbsp.
boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1" cubes 1 lb.
onion, chopped 1
extra-virgin olive oil 1 tbsp.
cloves garlic, minced 5
freshly minced ginger 1 tbsp.
ground turmeric 1/2 tsp.
ground cumin 2 tsp.
paprika 2 tsp.
garam masala 2 tsp.
cayenne pepper 1 tsp.
can crushed tomatoes 1 (28-oz.)
heavy cream 1/2 c. plus 2 tbsp.
Kosher salt 1 tbsp.
Freshly chopped cilantro, for garnish 1/2 c.
Rice or naan, for serving 4

Step 1:DIRECTIONS In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add chicken and cook, flipping once, until golden and no longer pink, 8 minutes per side.
Step 2:Transfer to a plate. Add onion to skillet and cook until soft, about 5 minutes. Add garlic, ginger, and spices and cook until fragrant, 1 minute. Add tomatoes and simmer until thickened, about 15 minutes.
Step 3: Add heavy cream and chicken and simmer until warmed through, 5 minutes more. Season with salt.
Step4: Garnish with cilantro and serve alongside rice or naan, if using.

Tandoori Chicken

  • Time: 2Hours 40Mins.
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Greek yogurt 1 c.
Juice of lime 1
clove garlic, minced 1
freshly grated ginger 1 tbsp.
garam masala 1 tsp.
ground turmeric 1/2 tsp.
Kosher salt 1
Pinch of cayenne pepper 1
chicken legs 2 lb.

Step 1: In a large bowl, whisk together yogurt, lime juice, garlic, ginger, garam masala, and turmeric and season with salt and cayenne.
Step 2: Add chicken and toss until fully coated. Let marinate in the fridge at least 2 hours.
Step 3: When ready to bake, preheat oven to 400º and line a baking sheet with a wire rack. Bake chicken 20 minutes, then flip and bake 20 minutes more. Heat broiler and broil until skin is crispy, 2 minutes..

Indian Butter Chicken

  • Time:1Hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients
For the chicken and marinade		 Quantity
Boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into roughly 1-inch cubes 1 pound
full-fat plain yogurt 1/4 cup
olive oil 1 tbsp
ginger garlic paste 1 tbsp
dried coriander 1 tspn
Kashmiri Lal Mirch 1 tspn
cumin 1 tspn
Salt to taste
Ingredients
For the sauce 		Quantity
whole almonds 8 to 10
whole cashews 8 to 10
unsalted butter, divided 2 tablespoons
ginger garlic paste 1 tbsp
can diced tomatoes 1 (15-ounce)
sugar 1 tbsp
dried coriander 2 tablespoons
ground garam masala 1 1/2 tbsp
ground Kashmiri Lal Mirch 1/2 tsp
salt 1/2 tsp
water 1 cup
heavy cream 1/4 cup
Kasuri Methi 1/2 tsp

Step 1: Marinate the chicken:
Bring the chicken and the rest of the marinade ingredients together in a large bowl. Using your hands, massage the marinade into the chicken.
Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or up to overnight.
Step 2: Soak cashews and almonds:
Soak cashews and almonds in water for at least an hour to (or up to overnight) before preparing the rest of the recipe.
Step 3: Cook the Chicken:
Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Drizzle a teaspoon of oil to the pan.
Working in batches, remove chicken from the marinade and grill them on the pan for 3 to 5 minutes on each side, until the chicken is cooked through (clear juice runs out of it when you cut into it). Do not crowd the pan.
Once cooked, transfer the chicken into a separate dish. Cover and set aside for later. Step 4:Start making the sauce:
 Melt 1 1/2 tablespoons of the butter in a medium sized Dutch oven or a skillet on medium low heat. As soon as the butter melts, add the ginger garlic paste followed by sugar. Sauté for 1 to 2 minutes.
Stir in the tomato and cook for 8 to 10 minutes on medium high heat, until all the extra liquid is cooked off. The sauce should be a loose, paste-like consistency.
Add coriander, garam masala, Kashmiri Lal Mirch, and salt. Stir and cook on medium heat for 2 to 3 minutes. Turn off the heat.
Step 5:Puree the sauce with the nuts:
Transfer the paste to a blender or the bowl of a food processor. Add the soaked cashews and almonds. Blend into a smooth paste.
Step 6:Finish the dish:
Return the paste to the cooking pan over medium heat. Add the water to the paste and stir to make a smooth sauce.
As the sauce begins to boil, add the grilled chicken and heavy cream. Stir well. Cover the pan and simmer for 5 minutes.
Step 7:Rest the butter chicken:
Turn off the heat and uncover the pan. Stir in the remaining 1/2 tablespoon butter and the kasuri methi. Cover again, and let the dish rest for 8 to 10 minutes so the flavor of kasuri methi can marry into the butter chicken.Source: Simplyrecipes.com
